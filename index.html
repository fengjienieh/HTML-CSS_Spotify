About the artist
Hikaru Utada

9,204,877 monthly litseners

Hikaru Utada's debut single “Automatic/time will tell” released on December 9, 1998 and sold over two million copies, catapulting them to top-artist-status at age 15. Their first album, First Love, released a few months later and marked the highest number of CDs sold in Japan, a record that remains unbroken to this day. All albums that followed have topped the charts.
